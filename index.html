About

Software Engineering and open source projects that I've
done or participated in.

There are reports and documentaions of the projects.

and discussions on the papers of the related fields
that I've read. The blogs also include some of my learning
notes on the significant concepts in the engineering
(application) field.

Since the articles are stored structured in a retrospective
way, they are easy to be retrieved and be able to concisely
displayed to the readers.

The website well-come all readers who are interested in the
topics of these articles. Enjoy the reading!
